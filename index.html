About

    I'm a Ph.D. student advised by Professor Leonidas J. Guibas at Stanford University. My research interests span across 3D vision, robotics, and computer graphics. My PhD thesis is on Category-Level Object Perception for Physical Interaction. Please feel free to contact me if you are interested in my research or potential collaborations.

    Prior to joining Stanford, I got my bachelor degree in the department of Microelectronics and Nanoelectronics from Tsinghua University.
